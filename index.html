Ottoman Timeline

Osman I (1299-1326)

Title: Osman Gazi or Kara Osman
Achievements: Founder of the Ottoman Empire, established the foundation for future expansion.

Orhan I (1326-1362)

Title: Orhan Gazi
Achievements: Expanded the empire into Europe, strengthened the state's administrative structure.

Murad I (1362-1389)

Title: Hüdavendigâr
Achievements: Conquered parts of the Balkans, established the Janissary corps.

Bayezid I (1389-1402)

Title: Yıldırım (The Thunderbolt)
Achievements: Continued expansion into Europe and Asia, faced Timur's invasion and was captured at the Battle of Ankara.
